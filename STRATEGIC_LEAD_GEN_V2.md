## The 6-Phase Process

### Phase 1: Build Prospect Company List

**Objective**: Create organized list of potential companies

**Process**:

3. Organize prospects systematically

**Segments** (10 total):

**Regions** (3 primary):

**Output**: Organized prospect list
```
Music Publishers - North America:
- Company A (not in database)
- Company B (not in database)

Music Publishers - Europe:
- Company C (not in database)

Record Labels - North America:
- Company D (not in database)
...
```

**Selection Criteria**:
- NOT in database (guaranteed new)
- Recent news/activity (2024-2026)
- Mid-to-large size
- Diverse segments and regions

### Phase 2: Deep Research on Selected Companies

**Objective**: Understand each company deeply

**Company Research**:
- Visit website thoroughly (About, Services, News pages)
- Current approach to rights management
- Technology stack (if mentioned)
- Pain points and challenges
- Recent news, expansions, acquisitions
- Company size and scale
- Geographic coverage

**Problem Identification**:
- What specific problems do they face?
- Manual processes mentioned?
- Scaling challenges?
- Multi-territory complexity?
- Technology gaps?
- Compliance issues?
- Revenue leakage?

**Contact Research**:
- Find 2-3 potential contacts
- Target decision-makers (CEO, CTO, VP Rights, VP Operations, etc.)
- Verify current employment
- Check LinkedIn profiles
- Find recent activity
- Identify responsibilities
- Determine decision authority

**Best Contact Selection**:
- Budget authority
- Cares about identified problems
- Involved in technology decisions
- Likely to respond

### Phase 3: Validation

**Objective**: Verify lead quality

**Company Website Validation**:
- ✅ Accessible and active
- ✅ Company name matches
- ✅ Industry confirmed
- ✅ Legitimate business

**Contact LinkedIn Validation**:
- ✅ Profile exists and is real
- ✅ Name matches exactly
- ✅ Current employment confirmed
- ✅ Title matches
- ✅ Active profile

**Employment Verification**:
- ✅ Search confirms connection
- ✅ Recent activity visible
- ✅ Company page exists

**If validation fails**: Select alternate contact or skip company

### Phase 4: Problem-Solution Email Creation

**Objective**: Create highly relevant, value-focused emails

**Email Structure**:

1. **Opening** - Specific observation about their business
2. **Problem Identification** - List 3 specific problems they face
3. **Solution Presentation** - How Noctil solves each problem
4. **Results** - Specific metrics and outcomes
5. **Social Proof** - Similar company example
6. **Call-to-Action** - Low-pressure meeting request
7. **P.S.** - Insightful question about their situation

**Example Email**:
```
Subject: Solving Multi-Territory Rights Tracking at [Company]

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company] recently expanded into Asian markets. Based on 
your catalog of 50,000+ songs, you're likely facing challenges with:

• Manual rights tracking across multiple territories
• Compliance with different copyright laws per country
• Revenue leakage from missed royalty collections

Noctil solves these challenges by:

Automated Rights Tracking: Track rights across 50+ territories 
automatically
→ Result: Reduce manual work by 80%, eliminate tracking errors

Unified Global Platform: Single source of truth for all territories
→ Result: Real-time visibility, faster decision-making

Comprehensive Collection: Automated royalty matching and collection
→ Result: Increase revenue capture by 25%

For example, [Similar Publisher] used Noctil to manage their Asian 
expansion and increased their royalty collection by 30% in 6 months.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss how we could help 
[Company] optimize your multi-territory operations?

Best regards,
Jacob Varghese
Noctil
www.noctil.com

P.S. How are you currently handling the complexity of different 
copyright laws across Asian territories?
```

**Key Principles**:
1. **Problem-First** - Start with their specific problems
2. **Solution-Focused** - Show exactly how Noctil solves each
3. **Results-Driven** - Include specific metrics
4. **Relevant** - Reference their specific situation
5. **Value-Oriented** - Focus on value creation
6. **Concise** - Under 150 words
7. **Actionable** - Clear call-to-action

### Weekly Analysis
```python
from leads_database import LeadsDatabase
import json

db = LeadsDatabase()

# Export and analyze
db.export_to_json()

# Analyze by segment
segments = {}
regions = {}

# Load export and count
with open(str(Path.home() / '.openclaw' / 'workspace' / 'leads_export.json')) as f:
    data = json.load(f)
    
for company in data['companies']:
    seg = company.get('industry_segment', 'Unknown')
    reg = company.get('region', 'Unknown')
    segments[seg] = segments.get(seg, 0) + 1
    regions[reg] = regions.get(reg, 0) + 1

print("Leads by Segment:")
for seg, count in sorted(segments.items(), key=lambda x: -x[1]):
    print(f"  {seg}: {count}")

print("\nLeads by Region:")
for reg, count in sorted(regions.items(), key=lambda x: -x[1]):
    print(f"  {reg}: {count}")
```
