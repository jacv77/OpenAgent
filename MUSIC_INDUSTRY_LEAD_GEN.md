```

    ↓

    ↓
Extract decision-maker contact information
    ↓
Generate company summary:
  - What they do in the music industry
  - Current rights management approach
  - Technology stack and systems
  - Territory coverage and catalog size
  - Recent news, funding, or growth
    ↓
Generate profile summary:
  - Role and responsibilities
  - Experience in rights management
  - Industry connections and reputation
  - Technology adoption mindset
  - Decision-making authority
    ↓

  - How noctil.com can help their specific needs
  - Relevant features for their segment
  - Benefits for their business
    ↓

    ↓
Track sent leads to avoid duplicates
    ↓
Send completion notification via Telegram
```

- Active in rights management or licensing

### Red Flags ❌
- Defunct or inactive company

- Consumer-focused (not B2B)
- No decision-maker contact available
- Very small operation with no growth potential
