## Why These Roles?

### Technology Leaders (CTO, CIO, VP Tech)
- **Decision Authority**: Direct control over technology investments
- **Pain Points**: System integration, scalability, efficiency
- **Noctil Value**: Technical infrastructure, API integration, automation

### Operations Leaders (COO, VP Ops)
- **Decision Authority**: Process improvement and efficiency
- **Pain Points**: Manual work, operational bottlenecks
- **Noctil Value**: 80% reduction in manual work, automation

### Rights Management Leaders
- **Decision Authority**: Rights administration systems
- **Pain Points**: Multi-territory tracking, compliance, accuracy
- **Noctil Value**: Automated rights tracking, real-time updates

### Metadata Leaders
- **Decision Authority**: Data quality and standards
- **Pain Points**: Metadata accuracy, standardization, integration
- **Noctil Value**: Industry standards (DDEX, CWR), data quality

### Business Development Leaders
- **Decision Authority**: Strategic partnerships and vendor selection
- **Pain Points**: Technology partnerships, integration capabilities
- **Noctil Value**: API connectivity, partnership opportunities

## Targeting Strategy

### Primary Focus (70% of leads)
- C-Level: CEO, CTO, CIO, COO
- VP/SVP: Technology, Operations, Rights Management

### Secondary Focus (20% of leads)
- VP/SVP: Metadata, Business Development, Engineering
- Director/Head: Rights, Technology, Operations

### Tertiary Focus (10% of leads)
- Senior Managers: Rights Management, Metadata, Business Development
- Only if they have clear decision authority
